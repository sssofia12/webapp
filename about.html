About me

Hello! I am Sofía, a final year student of Audiovisual Design and Illustration at UDIT.

I combine my skills in graphic design and my knowledge in illustration to bring fresh ideas and unique perspectives to each project. I work from the search for solid concepts and creative research, always with the aim of enriching my proposals with visual narrative and authenticity. I am especially interested in design in the cultural field.

My other great interest is branding, since it combines storytelling, research and creativity, developing visual and conceptual solutions that generate an impact.
